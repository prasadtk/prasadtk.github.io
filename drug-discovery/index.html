Overview

The drug discovery pipeline is a complex, multi-stage process that transforms scientific research into life-saving medications. It involves identifying disease targets, discovering and optimizing compounds, conducting rigorous testing, and navigating regulatory pathways. This knowledge base provides detailed insights into each stage, highlighting methodologies, technologies, challenges, and real-world examples.
